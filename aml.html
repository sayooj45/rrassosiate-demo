Compliance Services in UAE

The UAE has significantly strengthened its AML and CFT framework. RR Associates helps businesses implement robust AML programs, meet CBUAE requirements, and avoid severe penalties.

Why AML Compliance Matters

Protecting Your Business
from Regulatory Risk

The UAE made AML compliance a national priority after its FATF grey-listing in 2022 and removal in 2024. Businesses such as accountants, real estate firms, lawyers, and precious metals dealers now face strict obligations.

Non-compliance can lead to heavy fines, prosecution, license cancellation, and reputational damage. RR Associates provides practical end-to-end AML compliance support tailored to your risk profile.

  • Business Risk Assessment (BRA)
  • Customer Risk Assessment (CRA)
  • AML / CFT Policies & Controls
  • CDD & Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)
  • Suspicious Transaction Reporting (STR)
  • goAML Registration Support
  • UBO Register Compliance
  • Annual AML Program Reviews
