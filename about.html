India
INDIA — THE FOUNDATION

Arun Associates

Established 2006 · Kochi, Kerala

Arun Associates was founded in Kochi in 2006 with a clear mission — to provide practical, reliable, and affordable accounting and taxation services to businesses and individuals across India.

Over nearly two decades, the firm built a reputation for integrity, precision, and genuine client care — serving SMEs, startups, and individual professionals across Kerala, Chennai, Mumbai, and Palakkad. The practice grew beyond accounting to encompass GST advisory, HR services, internship training, and business compliance.

Today, Arun Associates remains one of India's trusted regional accounting partners — known for bridging the gap between technical compliance and real business understanding.

UAE — THE EXPANSION

RR Associates FZ LLC

Dubai, United Arab Emirates

RR Associates FZ LLC was established as the UAE extension of this trusted Indian legacy — bringing the same standards of excellence, the same commitment to clients, and the same depth of financial expertise to the Gulf market.

The UAE demanded a new level of specialisation — VAT compliance, Corporate Tax, free zone structuring, and advisory for an internationally diverse client base. RR Associates was built precisely for this: a firm that understands both the technical demands of UAE regulation and the human realities of running a business in one of the world's most dynamic economies.

What started in Kochi now serves entrepreneurs, family offices, and scaling enterprises across the UAE — carrying forward the same values that have defined the Arun Associates name for nearly 20 years.
